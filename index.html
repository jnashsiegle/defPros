“We Train You to Survive the Worst Day of Your Life”

We train civilian and law enforcement personnel, schools and school teachers, businesses,churches and their employees and staff to survive the worst day of their lives. Our hands-on training, classes and seminars are structured to give you the best learning experience available.

In addition, and unique to DefPros, is our Conflict Resolution service available to our clients that can help head off employee or staff disgruntlement which can turn into anger and violence.

Choose from a variety training classes available.

DefPros offers classes ranging from Basic to Advanced Pistol, License to Carry to Advanced Carry, Active Shooter for churches, schools and businesses to Force on Force training. All our services can be taught locally or at your location. Our instructor cadre comes from the law enforcement and military environments and brings an impressive array of years of experience, certifications, and knowledge to the table.
